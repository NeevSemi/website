Why On-Premise

Your IP never leaves your walls.

Semiconductor design data is among the most sensitive IP in the world. Sending proprietary RTL, netlist, or timing data to a cloud LLM provider isn't just a compliance risk — it's an existential one. LLMLocal keeps every prompt and response inside your firewall.

With LLMLocal, you get the power of frontier-scale language models with the security posture of a fully air-gapped system — optimized for the GPU infrastructure you already own.

  • Air-gapped deployment behind your firewall

  • GPU-optimized inference for semiconductor workloads

  • Fine-tuning on proprietary design data

  • Full compliance with ITAR and export controls
