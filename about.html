About NeevSemi

Making AI-native semiconductor design accessible to every chip team.

The Problem

The AI Rush That's Breaking Chip Design

The semiconductor industry is in the grip of an agentic AI gold rush. Every chip design team — from RTL generation to verification to DRC analysis — is under board-level pressure to "show AI wins," and fast. But speed without architecture is producing a generation of fragile, economically unsustainable workflows that are quietly breaking in production. The numbers are sobering: RAND Corporation's 2025 analysis of over 2,400 enterprise AI initiatives found that 80% fail to deliver intended business value, while S&P Global reports that 42% of companies scrapped most of their AI initiatives in 2025 — up from just 17% the year before.

The economics are particularly brutal for agentic systems. Gartner has documented that agentic AI workflows consume 5 to 30 times more tokens per task than standard interactions, turning a workflow that costs pennies in a demo into thousands of dollars per month at production scale. Yet most teams hardcode to a single LLM provider without benchmarking, cost guardrails, or any mechanism to adapt when better or cheaper models emerge — and in this market, they emerge daily.

The result is quick-fix prototypes shipped as production, with no closed-loop monitoring for model drift, API changes, or runaway costs. Gartner predicts over 40% of agentic AI projects will be canceled by the end of 2027. In semiconductors, where IP sensitivity, time-to-tape-out, and design correctness carry existential stakes, getting agentic AI wrong isn't just expensive — it sets entire programs back.

Our Approach

Agents for the Agents

NeevSemi — "Neev" (नींव), Sanskrit for "foundation" — exists to solve precisely this gap. We don't build chip design agents. We build foundational infrastructure agents that make agentic AI workflows enterprise-ready: optimized, observable, and economically sustainable.

Our platform, NeevDisha, starts where most solutions skip — by systematically analyzing your use cases, selecting domain-specific benchmarks for each, and building validated interfaces to the agents driving those workflows. At its core sits an optimization engine that continuously matches LLM configurations to user-defined criteria across both performance and cost, ensuring you never pay frontier prices for commodity tasks. And because the LLM landscape shifts faster than any team can manually track, a closed-loop agent automatically re-evaluates, re-benchmarks, and reconfigures your agentic tool stack as models improve, costs change, or requirements evolve.

No manual re-tuning, no vendor lock-in, no silent failures.

With NeevSemi, semiconductor teams can operationalize agentic AI for true enterprise usage — scaling confidently from proof-of-concept to production without the brittle architectures and runaway costs that have derailed the industry's first wave of AI adoption.
