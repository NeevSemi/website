We built NeevSemi for semiconductor teams who take data security seriously. This policy is written plainly — no legalese traps. If you have questions, email hello@neevsemi.com.

1. Who We Are

NeevSemi India Private Limited ("NeevSemi", "we", "our", "us") is a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, with CIN U62099KA2026PTC221452, having its registered office at No. 5M-627, 3rd Floor, MS Plaza, Banaswadi, Bangalore North, Karnataka 560043, India.

We develop AI infrastructure products for the semiconductor industry — including NeevDisha, LLMScoreBench, LLMGateway, and LLMLocal. This Privacy Policy applies to our website at neevsemi.com and any interactions you have with us through it.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide directly

When you fill out our contact form, request a demo, or email us, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Work email address
  • Company name
  • Your message or inquiry

We do not collect payment information on this website. Product licensing and billing are handled through separate agreements.

2.2 Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, our servers may log standard technical information including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, and pages visited. This information is used solely for diagnosing technical issues and understanding general traffic patterns. We do not use third-party analytics trackers or advertising cookies.

2.3 Information we do NOT collect

We do not collect sensitive personal data as defined under the IT (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011, through this website.

LLMLocal users: When you deploy LLMLocal on-premise, your design data, prompts, and model outputs remain entirely within your own infrastructure. NeevSemi never receives, accesses, or transmits any of that data. It never leaves your network.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiries, demo requests, and support questions
  • Send you information about NeevSemi products you have requested
  • Fulfil contractual obligations under product agreements
  • Comply with applicable legal requirements
  • Improve our website and services based on aggregate usage patterns

We do not use your information to send unsolicited marketing emails. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable (including for individuals in the European Economic Area), we process personal data on the following bases:

  • Consent — when you submit our contact form, you consent to us using your information to respond to your request.
  • Legitimate interests — for website analytics and improving our services, provided these interests are not overridden by your rights.
  • Legal obligation — where we must retain records to comply with Indian corporate or tax law.

5. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and related correspondence for up to 3 years from the date of last interaction, unless you request deletion sooner. Server access logs are retained for up to 90 days for security and diagnostic purposes.

If you enter into a product agreement with us, data retention terms specific to that agreement will apply.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We may use email hosting or CRM software to manage communications. These providers process data only on our behalf and under our instructions.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority in India or another applicable jurisdiction.
  • Business transfer: If NeevSemi is acquired or merges with another entity, personal data may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to equivalent privacy protections.

7. Cookies

Our website uses only essential session cookies required for basic functionality. We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking cookies, or third-party analytics tools that set persistent cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; this will not affect your ability to use the website.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data, subject to our legal retention obligations
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format where technically feasible

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@neevsemi.com with the subject line "Privacy Request". We will respond within 30 days.

9. Data Security

We apply industry-standard security measures to protect the information we hold, including encrypted email transmission, access controls, and secure server configurations. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

For detailed information about the security posture of our products, see our Security page.

10. International Transfers

NeevSemi is based in India. If you contact us from outside India, your information will be transferred to and processed in India. By submitting information through our website, you consent to this transfer. We take steps to ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection law.

11. Children's Privacy

Our website and products are intended for business professionals and are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that we have done so, we will delete it promptly.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated via a notice on our homepage. Continued use of our website after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
