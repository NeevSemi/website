What it does

One gateway. Every LLM. Every EDA tool.

LLMGateway sits between your semiconductor EDA tools and any LLM provider — routing calls intelligently, enforcing rate limits, caching responses, and producing the audit logs your compliance team needs.

  • Multi-provider routing with intelligent fallbacks

  • Rate limiting, caching, and cost controls

  • Semantic logging for compliance & audit

  • Native EDA tool integration plugins

Smart Routing
Routes to the optimal provider based on task type, cost envelope, and real-time availability. Automatic fallback when a provider degrades.
Compliance Logging
Every prompt and response is semantically logged with metadata. Full audit trail for IP-sensitive semiconductor workflows.
EDA-Native Plugins
Drop-in integration plugins for leading EDA toolchains. Your flows don't change — LLMGateway plugs in transparently.
